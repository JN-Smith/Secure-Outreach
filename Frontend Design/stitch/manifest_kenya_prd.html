# Manifest Kenya Outreach Data System - PRD ## Project Context Purpose-driven outreach data system for Manifest Kenya under the spiritual oversight of Phaneroo Ministries International. A mobile-friendly, enterprise-grade system that feels personal to the ministry, grounded in biblical outreach, and intuitive for field use. ## Brand & UI/UX Identity * **Primary Tone:** Personal, disciplined, Word-focused, clear, and bold. * **Visual Palette:** * Base: Clean White (#FFFFFF) * Primary Accent: Deep Navy/Royal Blue or Dark Charcoal (Trust/Clarity) * Secondary Accent: Warm Gold or Rich Burgundy (Spiritual Impact/Warmth) * **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Montserrat or Inter) with high legibility. * **UI Style:** Minimal, no commercial clichés, simple forms, large touch targets. ## MVP Scope ### 1. Authentication & Access Control * Admin-created accounts via invite links. * Role-based access: Evangelist, Pastor, Admin. ### 2. Outreach Data Entry (Evangelists) * Fields: Name, Contact (with consent), Salvation Status (Saved/Not Saved/Unsure), Location, Student Status (Yes/No), Follow-up status, Notes. * Automatic: Timestamp and Evangelist ID. ### 3. Dashboards * **Evangelist:** Total reached, breakdown by salvation status, recent entries. * **Pastor:** Total reached, total saved, pending follow-ups. ### 4. Data Storage & Filtering * Backend: PostgreSQL. * Filters: By salvation status, by evangelist. ## Success Criteria * Easy data recording in real outreach contexts. * Reliable metrics for leadership oversight. * Clean, mobile-optimized UI.
