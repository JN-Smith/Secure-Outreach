Goal: Build a secure outreach app for volunteers and church staff to record and follow up with people they reach.

1. Authentication & User Accounts

Users can sign up once with email or phone and a password.

Supports automatic login on trusted devices.

Secure authentication with role-based access:

Outreach worker: can record and view their contacts only

Pastor/Leader: can view aggregated reports

Admin: full access

Password reset and email verification included.

2. Core Feature – Recording Contacts

Create a form for outreach entries with the following fields:

Personal Information

Full Name

Phone Number / Contact Details (consent checkbox)

Gender (optional)

Age Range (optional)

Spiritual & Church Info

Church they attend (if any)

Born again? (Yes / No / Unsure)

Discipleship status (Done / In Progress / Not Started)

Baptized? (Yes / No / Unsure)

Location & Background

Place of residence (town/area)

Is the person a student? (Yes / No)

If yes: Institution name, course, year of study

Follow-Up & Pastoral Care

Preferred method of follow-up (Call / WhatsApp / Visit / Church Invitation)

Best time to contact

Prayer requests

Notes / Observations

Follow-up status (New / Needs Follow-up / Actively Discipling / Connected to Church / Not Interested)

3. Additional Features

Tags for categorization (Student, New Believer, Campus Outreach, etc.)

Search & filter by location, church, follow-up status, student/non-student

Timeline or activity log for follow-ups

Reminders for scheduled follow-ups

Dashboard summary:

Total people reached

Pending follow-ups

Students reached

People connected to church

Export reports as CSV/PDF

4. Privacy & Ethics

Consent acknowledgment when saving contact details

Secure data storage (encrypted)

Role-based access control for church staff

5. Design & Experience

Clean, mobile-first UI

Easy, fast data entry

Offline support with auto-sync when online

Faith-based, respectful, and welcoming tone

6. Tech Stack Recommendations (for Bolt AI)

Frontend: Bolt low-code UI components

Backend: Bolt database + workflows

Authentication & login: Bolt Auth component

Dashboard: Bolt charts & tables
